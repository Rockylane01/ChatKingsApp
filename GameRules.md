GAME RULES

Points System
1. Everyone starts each week with 1,000 points — no real money, ever.
2. Points reset every Sunday at midnight; you can never go below 0.
3. Points are wagered on predictions created by the King.
4. Winners divide the pot in proportion to what they wagered — bet more, win more (if right).

The King
1. The King is whoever holds the most points at the start of the week.
2. The King must place the first wager before a prediction opens to the group.
3. The King must take a side on a bet, or else they lose 10 points
4. If someone surpasses the King's point total mid-week, the crown transfers immediately.

Predictions
1. Each prediction has a question and 2 answer options.
2. Pick one option and wager any amount between 50% and 150% of the initial bet.
3. Initial bet must be a minimum of 20 points.
4. If a prediction isn’t bet against, 20 points are added to the pot and points are redistributed proportionally

Minority Bonus
1. Pick the correct answer AND be on the side that had fewer total points wagered than all other options combined, earn a 20% bonus payout

Three-Strikes (Daily Limit System)
1. Each strike is earned by losing a wager — you get 3 strikes per day per  chat before you aren’t allowed to continue betting in that chat. Reset at midnight
2. Strike 1 (yellow): 1 wager used. 2 remain today.
3. Strike 2 (orange): 2 wagers used. 1 remains 
4. Strike 3 (red): Daily limit reached — predictions locked until midnight reset.
5. Strikes reset to 0 at midnight every day.

Weekly Resets
1. All point totals reset to 1,000 every Sunday at midnight — a fresh, equal start.
2. The King crown goes to whoever had the highest points at the end of week; ties go to the prior King.
3. Your all-time record (wins, minority bonuses, times crowned) is preserved in Activity forever.
